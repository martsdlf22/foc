This course serves as an initial exploration into the foundational principles of Computer Science, aiming to establish a base for students to build upon in subsequent Computer Science courses. It offers a comprehensive overview of the discipline, covering various topics that enable students to comprehend the capabilities of computer systems. These topics include computer architecture, operating systems, computer networks, software and software development, data organization, visualization, and the potential applications of computing machines.
