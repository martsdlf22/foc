This course serves as an introductory exploration into the core principles of Computer Science, aiming to establish a solid groundwork for subsequent advanced courses in the field. It encompasses a comprehensive overview of the discipline, delving into key areas such as computer architecture, operating systems, computer networks, software development, data organization, visualization, and the expansive capabilities inherent in computing machines. The intention is to equip students with a holistic understanding of the intricacies within the realm of Computer Science, fostering a solid foundation for their academic journey in the subject.
